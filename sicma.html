SICMA

B2B Demand Generation & SEO

SICMA Ecuador · B2B Sustainability Consultancy · 2023–2024

Built SICMA's full marketing function from scratch, generating 121 qualified B2B leads in 2023 and achieving a CPL of ~$10 USD.

Demand Generation SEO / Growth B2B Marketing

The Challenge

SICMA Ecuador, an early-stage B2B sustainability consultancy (14 people), had a strong service offering but no marketing function, low digital presence, and no pipeline. The company relied entirely on direct sales and word of mouth. I was their first marketing hire with a single mission: build the engine that would bring qualified leads to the commercial team for their regulatory services and carbon neutrality certification program.

My Role

Marketing Team Lead with end-to-end ownership of strategy, execution, and team management, reporting directly to the CEO.

Approach

  • Conducted a full digital presence audit and developed a content strategy, social media strategy, and SEO optimization plan as the foundation for all subsequent work.
  • Redesigned the company website with improved UX, copywriting, and technical SEO, optimizing headings, images, and keywords to target sustainability managers, environmental managers, and C-suite decision-makers.
  • Built a full-funnel demand generation strategy using Google Ads and Meta Ads, defining ICPs and buyer personas per industry segment and integrating the pipeline into HubSpot for lead tracking and qualification.
  • Organized exclusive bottom-of-funnel events; first CEO breakfast targeting sustainability directors, and coordinated webinars to nurture and convert qualified leads.
  • Led the go-to-market foundation for SICMA International: bilingual EN/ES website, keyword and market research for the US market, and initial Google Ads and SEO setup to build international awareness.

  • 🎯 Closed pipeline of $11K attributed to digital leads within the first quarter of 2024 - Google Ads investment of $200 USD/month
  • 📈 170% growth in new users (Sep 2023 - Jan 2024) + 77% growth in web sessions
  • 🧲 ~20 leads/month with a 65% qualification rate once the full-funnel strategy was in place, with ~$10 CPL (Google Ads)
  • 🌎 New market entered. SICMA International launched in English and Spanish for the US market

Key Learnings

  • Positioning beats budget. The highest-ROI campaigns weren't the biggest, they were the most precisely targeted.
  • Iterate fast. The organic growth came from dozens of small optimizations, not one big move.
  • Translation is the hardest B2B skill. Converting technical sustainability frameworks into C-suite messaging requires as much strategy as the campaign itself.
  • Enablement is as important as lead gen. Leads only became pipeline when the sales team had the right scripts, decks, and objection-handling tools.
